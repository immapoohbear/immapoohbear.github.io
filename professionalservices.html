Network Planning and Optimization engineers

Our engineers creates, plans, and implements an organization's network system's overall strategic goals. Evaluates and advises adjustments to present and future network requirements in order to satisfy the goals of the company.

They also focus on taking an existing design and improving making it stronger, fast, more efficient, or more durable.It provides effective network life cycle management by offering end-to-end visibility and control of the network, resulting in better resource management, more efficiency, and lower costs.

Transmission Planners

Identify and assess transmission system reinforcements required to remedy reliability and operational difficulties, as well as to improve economic efficiency. Assess the adequacy and dependability of the organization's system. Identify and encourage the development of new, creative processes and instruments in the planning process.

Drive Test Engineers

A drive test engineer is a radio frequency expert who is in charge of keeping radio networks operational and efficient. A drive test engineer performs tests to ensure that radio frequency circuits and systems are working properly. They also inspect existing networks to ensure they are operating at peak efficiency.

IBS Solution Planner and Supervisor

NOC Resources

A NOC (network operation center) engineer is a specialist who monitors big computer networks and servers for problems from a centralized location or by satellite.

They assess problems, do troubleshooting and incident response on the system, and engage with field technicians to find a better solution to satisfy company goals or objectives.

 Their responsibilities are extensive. A NOC or Network Operations Center requires competence in network administration, network support, management, switching, and routing.
