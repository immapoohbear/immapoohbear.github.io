About Us

‘We Network LLC’ (WENET), is a rapidly growing Telecom network company with our operations spread across the the Middle East and South East Asian subcontinent regions. Our service offerings are Turnkey telecom infrastructure projects, Enterprise IT solutions and Alternative energy solutions.

Turnkey Telecom projects division is our Flagship division. In less than a decade WENET has built an enviable track record in executing very large telecom projects for leading telecom service providers in the region and for the world leaders of telecom network equipment manufacturers (OEMs) who have their operations in the region.

Having consolidated our solution offering in the telecom network design and implementation, we have expanded our operations into related domains of enterprise communications and alternative energy solutions.The company is headquartered in Knowledge Oasis Muscat and has its operations spread across Oman. The company has branches in UAE and India offering all the services.
